About me

Born and raised in Stockport. I moved across the penines in 2004 to study Business and Communication Technology at Leeds Metropolitan University. My first module was web design and I fell in love with it straight away. My dad sent me a copy of Macromedia Studio MX and I was away! Using Dreamweaver to build websites for friends and family, creating images with Fireworks and animating with Flash. Good times.

After University I worked as a graphic designer for a small food manufacturing company, responsible for designing brochures, newspaper ads, vehicle branding, food packaging... allsorts really. It was fun. I learned a lot about colour theory, imagery, typography, composition. Vital skills for any designer.

I then ventured into user interface design.
